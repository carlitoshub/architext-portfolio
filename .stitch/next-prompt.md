A project case study detail page for Giancarlo Banchi's PM portfolio. This is a deep-dive into a single project — "Cultural Center Renovation" for the Municipality of Florence.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
Design system: editorial European portfolio.
Background: warm parchment #F4F1EB. Text: near-black #1A1917.
Accent color: navy #1D3557 for CTAs and highlights.
Dividers: 1px lines in #D8D3CB — no shadows, borders only.
Typography: Cormorant Garamond (serif, large display) + Jost (sans, UI/body).
Hero and page titles in large Cormorant Garamond italic+bold.
Labels: tiny uppercase Jost with wide letter-spacing.
Layout: structured, grid-based, generous whitespace.
Hover states: light warm fill #EDE9E2.
Fixed top nav, 60px, with logo left and links right.
Buttons: navy filled (primary) or transparent outlined (secondary).
Overall mood: refined, precise, architectural — not flashy.

**Page Structure:**
1. Fixed top nav (logo left, Home / Archive / About / Contact links right)
2. Page header — large title "Cultural Center Renovation" with italic accent, client name "Municipality of Florence", year "2024", status pill "In Progress"
3. Two-column overview: left = project summary paragraph, right = key facts (Budget €2.4M, Duration 18 months, Team size 12, Role PM Lead)
4. Full-width divider section — quote or key stat highlight in navy background (accent)
5. Three-column methodology section — Challenge / Approach / Outcome cards with numbered labels
6. Deliverables / milestones list — table-style rows with phase name, deliverable, and status
7. CTA strip — "Back to Archive" secondary button + "Get in Touch" primary button
8. Footer consistent with rest of site
