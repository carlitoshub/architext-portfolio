# Giancarlo Banchi — Project Manager Portfolio

## Site Name
Giancarlo Banchi | PM Portfolio

## Pages

### Home
The landing page. Introduces Giancarlo as a project manager — his name, title, and a brief value proposition. Includes a hero section, a short summary of his expertise, and calls-to-action linking to his work and contact page.

### Archive
A chronological or filterable list of past and current projects. Each entry shows the project name, client or context, timeline, and a short description. Acts as the main portfolio showcase.

### About
A deeper look at Giancarlo's background, methodology, and professional philosophy. Covers his experience, skills, tools he uses, and what makes him effective as a PM.

### Contact
A simple, clean contact page with a form or direct contact details (email, LinkedIn). Encourages potential clients or collaborators to reach out.
